    • Vector

    For my vector project I wanted to build off of my original raster image but design more of the elements myself. Working with vectors was a lot more complicated and challenging that with pixels in Photoshop and I ran into a lot of problems while drawing the bird and gun. In my first pass I drew it as a single object and then ultimately had to go in and explode all of my lines and re-close them to define each individual object.
