    Animate/Flash

    My Animate project built off of my first two projects and I wanted to try animating my vector bird/gun - not an easy task! I drew each frame individually in order to create the illusion of flight. I ended up animating the two wings separately. The rest of the animation - the laser beam/explosions was all relatively straight forward and utilized motion/shape tweens.
